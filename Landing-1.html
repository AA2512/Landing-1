Our Hiring Process.

In our hiring process, the first step is to apply with a portfolio that clearly shows your skills. After this step, we will analyze your portfolio, and based on that we will have a short interview with you, where we will give you an overview of our company and the further project and process. After that, you will work on a team project where you will collaborate with the team and work on a real project. And this will also be fully paid, so your effort will not be in vain. Now we will do a project/performance evaluation and interview. If your performance fits our company, you will be selected. Selected candidates will receive an offer letter and then participate in the onboarding process. Welcome to Workskills.
